Customer Support – Beautyfilled Clothing

1. How Do I Place an Order?

Simply browse our website and click the "Add to List" button on the products you like. When you're ready, tap the green "Cart" button, fill in your details, and click "Send via WhatsApp". We'll confirm your order quickly!

2. How Do I Pay?

Once you place your order, we'll send you our bank account info on WhatsApp. Payment is made via EFT (Electronic Funds Transfer). We process your order after confirming payment.

3. How Much is Delivery?

Delivery costs depend on your location. Once you send us your address, we’ll calculate the courier fee and let you know the total cost before you pay.

4. Returns & Exchanges

We only accept returns if an item arrives damaged. Unfortunately, we do not offer exchanges for size or color. Please read item descriptions carefully before ordering.

5. Product Quality

We resell trusted brands like SHEIN and always aim for high-quality stock. However, as resellers, we don’t control the manufacturing process. If you're unsure about an item, feel free to ask us on WhatsApp.
